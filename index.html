Life of Marcus Aurelius

  • Born - 26 April 121 CE
  • Marcus Aurelius adopted by Antoninus Pius as successor. - 138 CE
  • Marcus Aurelius marries Faustina, daughter of Antoninus Pius. - 145 CE
  • Reign of Roman Emperor Marcus Aurelius. - 7 March 161 CE - 17 Mar 180 CE
  • Lucius Verus and Marcus Aurelius celebrate a shared triumph, both are hailed as Pater Patriae. - October 166 CE
  • Germanic tribes cross the Danube into the Roman Empire. - 168 CE
  • Lucius Verus dies and Marcus Aurelius rules alone. - March 169 CE - 177 CE
  • Marcus Aurelius leads campaigns against the Germanic tribes; writes his famous Meditations. - 170 CE - 180 CE
  • The Column of Marcus Aurelius and Faustina is erected in Rome. It depicts in relief sculpture the emperors' campaigns across the Danube. - 180 CE
